SMART PACKAGING

FAST. FAIR. FLEXIBLE.

Smart Packaging Solutions: Meeting the Trifecta of Protection, Sales, and Sustainability

Good and intelligent packaging has to meet a wide range of requirements and demands: It must protect. It must sell. And increasingly, it must also be sustainable and contribute to the circular economy. Paper-based packaging like corrugated board is very well suited to meeting all these requirements. Together with our long-standing partner, ELGRAF Ltd, we offer smart packaging solutions. Especially for particularly demanding products and the retail sector.

How do our customers benefit from this expertise and service?

ELGRAF is an independent producer and quick decision-maker. Customers benefit from low entry costs and a fast start-up time for new projects. Beyond this, ELGRAF offers short production and delivery times. And – as one of the frontrunners in this area – ELGRAF is specialized in sophisticated micro-flute solutions such as the E-, F- and N-flute. As PTG, we take on the consulting and entire handling of your projects and are your direct contact.
