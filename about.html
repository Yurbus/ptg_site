Empowering Your Vision: Two Decades of Expertise in Paper and Packaging Solutions

Since 2002, we have been advising and supporting our customers in the paper and packaging sector. With a dedicated team and professional partners, we offer contemporary graphic paper and packaging papers. Thanks to our close cooperation with ELGRAF, we are also able to offer smart packaging solutions for the retail sector at first hand.

Strategic Alliances, Global Reach: Elevating Excellence in Paper and Packaging Solutions

What sets us apart is our strategic partnership with leading paper producers and especially our close cooperation with ELGRAF Ltd., a passionate packaging producer dedicated to smart retail packaging solutions.

Thanks to our company headquarters in Neuchatel, Switzerland, we are able to professionally cover the entire European market and also act as the first point of contact and processing partner for the European customers of ELGRAF.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every printing and packaging project is realized with the utmost care for the planet, without compromising on quality or performance. We strive to be an active player in the transition to a circular economy, where all paper and packaging materials are regenerative and contribute to a closed-loop system.

Our Mission

Driven by a passion for sustainability, our mission is to support printing and packaging solutions that minimize waste and promote circular economies. We believe that packaging should not only preserve the integrity of products but also uphold the integrity of the environment. Through long-standing and trusting partnerships that support cutting-edge technologies and a commitment to eco-friendly practices, we are contributing to a future worth living.
