Cookie Policy

When you visit the Paper Trading Group "PTG" website, we automatically collect certain information using technologies such as cookies. This information helps us improve your experience and provide a more convenient use of our site.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website saves on your computer or mobile device when you visit it. They allow the site to remember your preferences and other information, making your use of the site more efficient and convenient for you.

How do we use cookies?

We use cookies for the following purposes:
  • Analytics: We collect data on how you use our site to understand how we can improve it for our users.
  • Functionality: Some cookies are necessary to provide functionality on our site, such as saving language settings or automatic login.
  • Advertising: We may use cookies to show you relevant advertisements for our products or services.

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Typically, you can configure your browser to reject all cookies or to notify you when a site tries to set a cookie. Please note that disabling cookies may lead to limited functionality on our site.

By continuing to use our website, you agree to our cookie policy as outlined in this notice.

If you have any questions regarding our cookie policy, please contact us at contact@papertrading.ch
