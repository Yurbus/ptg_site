Unveiling the Impact of Graphic Paper: Weaving Stories, Connecting Lives

Packaging paper, for example in the form of corrugated cardboard boxes, has to meet a wide range of requirements today: It must protect the packaged goods, it should offer an appealing - and protective - surface for impressive branding - and meanwhile customers, consumers and legislators are demanding a tangible contribution in terms of sustainability on top. Achieving all these goals at the same time is only possible with the right base material: really good packaging paper.

Contributing to ecology and economy

At PTG, we go beyond simply offering graphic paper; we actively contribute to the circular economy. Our long-standing partnership with LEIPA, the pioneer and European leader in 100% recycled LWC paper, reflects our dedication to sustainable practices, as LEIPA has consistently set the standard for environmentally conscious graphic paper production.

By prioritizing recycled materials, we do not only deliver top-notch quality but also play a vital role in reducing environmental impact together with our customers.

Strong partnership and shared responsibility

We believe in the power of partnership and shared responsibility. By choosing PTG as your partner for graphic papers, you actively participate in the circular economy, promoting a sustainable and eco-friendly approach to paper production. Join us in making a positive impact on the environment while enjoying the uncompromised quality of our graphic paper.
